IfcKinematicViscosityMeasure
============================
_IfcKinematicViscosityMeasure_ is a measure of the viscous resistance of a
medium to a moving body.  
  
Usually measured in m2/s.  
  
Type: REAL  
  
> HISTORY  New type in IFC2.0.
